Grilled Chicken

This grilled chicken recipe is simple and delicious! Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the spice mixture.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
