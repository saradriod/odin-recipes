French Fries

This French fries recipe is very easy to make and tastes better than takeout! It's basically potatoes, oil, and salt. For best results, use fresh potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Fry the potato strips in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. Season with salt and serve hot.
