Fried Rice

This chicken fried rice recipe is very easy to make and tastes better than takeout! It's basically cooked chicken, rice, soy sauce, and shredded egg stir-fried together. For best results, use leftover cooked rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked rice and stir to combine.
  5. Add the scrambled eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes until heated through.
